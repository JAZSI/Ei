Welcome to Ei Bot!

Ei is a versatile and fun anime-themed discord bot that can enhance any server's anime experience.

Invite the bot Join our Discord Server

Features

Search for anime and manga

Ei Bot can help you find information about anime and manga. Just use the appropriate command and Ei Bot will search the internet for you!

Moderation

Ei Bot can also help you moderate your Discord server. You can use commands to kick or ban users, delete messages, and more!

Animated emojis

With Ei Bot, you can use animated emojis in your messages even if you don't have a Nitro subscription.
